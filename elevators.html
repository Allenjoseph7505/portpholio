Capsule Elevators

MRL (Machine-Room-Less) Elevator

The revolutionary MRL (Machine-Room-Less) elevator is an innovative product that eliminates the need for a dedicated machine room, saving significant construction space, time, and materials. This design provides architects with greater flexibility and allows building owners to effectively utilize valuable space. The compact, gearless hoisting machine fits neatly within the hoistway itself.

The MRL Advantage

  • Space & Cost Savings: No machine room reduces construction costs and frees up building space.
  • Energy Efficient: The advanced gearless motor consumes up to 30% less energy than traditional systems.
  • Superior Comfort: A digital drive controller and advanced roping system ensure excellent traveling comfort with a smooth, quiet ride.
  • Maximum Safety & Reliability: Engineered for top-tier safety with reduced maintenance requirements.

Hydraulic & Car Elevators

We offer a range of powerful hydraulic elevator solutions suitable for both passengers and vehicles. These systems are ideal for low to mid-rise buildings and offer design flexibility since the machine room can be located remotely. Our specialized car elevators provide a robust solution for the vertical transportation of vehicles in showrooms, multi-level car parks, and industrial settings, with capacities from 500 to 3000 kg and beyond.

Hydraulic Solutions

  • Hole-Type: A direct suspension system with a simple piston, suitable for travels up to 6 meters.
  • Hole-Less Type: A side direct suspension with one or two pistons, ideal for large loads and short travels where drilling a jackhole is not feasible.
  • Car Transportation: Designed for industrial applications and car parks, providing safe and efficient vertical movement for LMVs.

Escalators & Stairlifts

Our escalators and moving walks are designed for high-traffic public spaces such as airports, subways, and shopping centers, providing smooth, safe, and continuous movement of people. For residential and private buildings, our premium stairlifts offer a safe and comfortable solution to improve mobility on any staircase, ensuring greater freedom and independence for the young and elderly alike.

Mobility for All

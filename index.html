About Stanley Industries Group

Stanley Industries Group is a diversified company with two core divisions dedicated to shaping a better future. STANLEY ENERGY POWER INDIA PVT. LTD. is our premier Solar EPC division, providing innovative clean energy solutions. Our mobility division, STANLEY ELEVATOR COMPANY, brings 25 years of specialized experience in vertical transportation, with an unwavering commitment to safety, quality, and robust performance.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, sustainable, and innovative solutions in energy and mobility that empower communities and protect our planet for future generations.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in the power and mobility sectors, recognized for our technological excellence, customer-centric approach, and dedication to a sustainable world.
